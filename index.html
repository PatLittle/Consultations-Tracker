Consulting with Canadians is Canada's one stop shop for finding Consultations.

This tool scans all Consultations from Consulting with Canadians which are listed as either Open or Planned and checks to ensure the profile page is an active link and tries each link every 4 hours. In addition to uptime monitoring, several handy reports are generated such as Consultations starting and ending soon, consultations overdue to start, consulations open beyond their end date, etc.
